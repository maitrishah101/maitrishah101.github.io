Quarantine4Kids provides free, engaging activities for K-12 students during the pandemic, while raising money for important causes. We have grown to 20 volunteers, provide classes M-F and have students from all over the country participating.

SWE++

SWE++ is a committee that I started under the Society of Women Engineers at Berkeley. Our goal is to provide quality computer science education to middle school girls in the area. Since Fall 2018, we've taught around 30 middle schoolers basic Python skills and provided them with a supportive, inclusive, and fun environment to learn in. SWE++ has grown to over 6 universities across the nation and has taught over 500 students and counting.
